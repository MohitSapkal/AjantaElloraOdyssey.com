About Eternal Trail

Our Story & Leadership

Discover Our Heritage

Eternal Trail was founded with a singular purpose: to showcase the rich and profound history of Maharashtra to the world. We specialize in curating luxury, immersive experiences across the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Ajanta and Ellora, as well as the historical city of Aurangabad and the geological marvel of Lonar Crater.

Our fleet of premium vehicles and team of multilingual, expert guides ensure that your journey is not just a vacation, but a comfortable and enlightening exploration of India's golden past.

Mission & Vision

"To deliver world-class travel experiences that celebrate our historical legacy while ensuring utmost comfort, safety, and personalization for our guests. We envision becoming the gold standard for heritage tourism in India."
