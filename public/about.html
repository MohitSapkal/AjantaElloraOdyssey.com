Our Story & Leadership

Discover Our Heritage

AjantaElloraOdyssey.com was founded with a singular purpose: to showcase the rich and profound history of Maharashtra to the world. We specialize in curating luxury, immersive experiences across the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Ajanta and Ellora, as well as the historical city of Aurangabad and the geological marvel of Lonar Crater.

Our fleet of premium vehicles and team of multilingual, expert guides ensure that your journey is not just a vacation, but a comfortable and enlightening exploration of India's golden past.
