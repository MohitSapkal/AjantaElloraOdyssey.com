Heritage & Devotion Trail

Ajanta - Ellora - Shirdi - Shani Shingnapur

4 Days / 3 Nights

Tour Overview

A thoughtfully curated journey blending the artistic heritage of Ajanta & Ellora with the spiritual devotion of Shirdi and Shani Shingnapur. This itinerary offers a balanced experience of culture, history, and faith, designed at a comfortable pace for families, senior travellers, and spiritual seekers.

Tour Highlights

  • UNESCO World Heritage Sites: Ajanta & Ellora
  • Spiritual centres: Shirdi & Shani Shingnapur
  • Balanced mix of heritage, history & devotion
  • Comfortable pacing with clean lunch stops
  • Suitable for families, senior citizens & spiritual travellers

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Ajanta Caves

Theme: The World of the Monks

Distance

~210 km (round trip)

Driving Time

~4.5–5 hours

Day Brief

Morning departure for Ajanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for ancient Buddhist murals and rock-cut monasteries carved into a dramatic river gorge. Guided exploration of selected caves gives insight into Buddhist art, philosophy, and monastic life. Lunch near the site, followed by return to the city. Evening at leisure.

Sightseeing Highlights

  • Ajanta Caves (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Key caves with murals, chaitya halls, and sculptures
Day 2

Ellora Caves & Daulatabad Fort

Theme: Monoliths and Empires

Distance

~60 km

Driving Time

~2 hours

Day Brief

Visit Ellora Caves, showcasing Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain monuments carved side by side. Focus on the iconic Kailasa Temple (Cave 16), a monolithic marvel. Post lunch, visit Daulatabad Fort, known for its strategic hilltop location and historic defence systems. Return to the city by evening.

Sightseeing Highlights

  • Ellora Caves (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Kailasa Temple (Cave 16)
  • Select Buddhist & Jain caves
  • Daulatabad Fort (partial climb optional)
Day 3

Shirdi (Sai Baba Temple)

Theme: Path of Devotion

Distance

~120 km

Driving Time

~2.5–3 hours

Day Brief

Drive to Shirdi, one of India’s most important pilgrimage centres. Visit the Sai Baba Samadhi Mandir, Dwarkamai, and Chavadi. Time for darshan and personal prayers. Evening aarti can be attended (subject to crowd conditions). Relaxed evening in Shirdi.

Sightseeing Highlights

  • Sai Baba Samadhi Mandir
  • Dwarkamai
  • Chavadi
  • Gurusthan
Day 4

Shani Shingnapur & Return

Theme: Faith & Living Traditions

Distance

~170 km total

Driving Time

~4–4.5 hours

Day Brief

Morning visit to Shani Shingnapur, famous for the open-air shrine of Lord Shani and the village’s unique tradition of homes without doors. After darshan, begin return journey to Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. Tour concludes by evening.

Sightseeing Highlights

  • Shani Shingnapur Temple
  • Village heritage and belief traditions
